My Story

Designing at the intersection of empathy & craft.

I'm a UX Designer with over 5 years of experience crafting digital products for healthcare, fintech, and enterprise. My work is grounded in research — I believe understanding people is the foundation of every great design decision.

I've worked across the full design lifecycle: from early discovery and user research to high-fidelity prototypes and design systems. I love collaborating closely with engineers and product managers to ship work that's both beautiful and functional.

Currently at Slalom, where I lead UX design for client-facing digital transformation projects.
